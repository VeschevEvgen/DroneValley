Presentation

Drone Valley



We are an international strategic association of Manufacturers, Financing and Partners in the field of military drones.

The mission is to strengthen the collective security of the democratic world through the development of an ecosystem of military drones.

Strategy

1st stage:
To accumulate an advanced experience and technologies of the 7th generation war in Ukraine and to integrate them into the countries of Eastern Europe.
2nd stage:
To transfer the achievements of the 1st Stage to other democratic countries.
3rd Stage:
To become an integral part of the European system of collective security.

Tsunami of drones


Eastern European Security Ecosystem built on the basis of national Drone Clusters.
Basic characteristics
1. Massiveness - the "Drone Swarm" principle, which is multiplied by dozens of national Drone Clusters.
2. Comprehensiveness - decentralized, but coordinated by Drone Valley, Eastern European R&D, production, infrastructure.
3. Integration - Tsunami Drones should become a part of national military programs and be integrated into the system of collective European security.
4. Power - in the case of an attack on the Participating Country Tsunami Drones, similarly to Art. 5 of the NATO Charter, the enemy is attacked in a single system by hundreds of thousands of different types of drones and accompanying infrastructure.
Advantages of participation together with Drone Valley
1. The priority of Drone Valley in providing its services.
2. The priority when merging into production Consortia, Partner Clusters, Financial Syndicates.
3. Zone of "free trade" among the Participants who are included in the Tsunami of Drones.
4. Work on a single global project for the protection of Eastern Europe.
5. Active Tsunami of Drones Participants receive Drone Valleys Options.
